# Day Overviews

## 1DAY
Covers the basics of hypothesis testing by distinguishing statistical significance from clinical significance, interpreting p-values, and using confidence intervals to judge effect size and precision.

## 2DAY
Covers independent-samples t-tests, including assumptions, unequal-variance handling with Welch's test, and interpreting effect size with Cohen's d.

## 3DAY
Covers categorical outcome analysis using risk, risk difference, risk ratio, odds ratio, and chi-square testing, with emphasis on when odds can mislead compared with risk.

## 4DAY
Covers logistic regression for binary outcomes, including log-odds, coefficient and odds-ratio interpretation, confounding adjustment, and model discrimination with AUC.
